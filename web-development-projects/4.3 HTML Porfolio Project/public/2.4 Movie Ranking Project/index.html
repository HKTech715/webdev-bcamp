The Best Movies Of All Time (according to Me)

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Godfather

The Godfather is a 1972 American epic gangster film[2] directed by Francis Ford Coppola,
who co-wrote the screenplay with Mario Puzo, based on Puzo's best-selling 1969 novel.

The Shawshank Redemptiom

Andy Dufresne (Tim Robbins) is sentenced to two consecutive life terms in prison
for the murders of his wife and her lover and is sentenced to a tough prison.
However, only Andy knows he didn't commit the crimes.

Step Brothers

Brennan Huff (Will Ferrell) and Dale Doback (John C. Reilly) have one thing in common:
they are both lazy, unemployed leeches who still live with their parents. When Brennan's mother
and Dale's father marry and move in together, it turns the overgrown boys' world upside down.
